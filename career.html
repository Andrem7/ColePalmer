Manchester City Academy (2010-2023)

Palmer joined Manchester City's academy at age 8 and progressed through their youth system. He made his first-team debut in 2020 and gradually established himself as a promising talent under Pep Guardiola.

Transfer to Chelsea (September 2023)

In a £40 million move on transfer deadline day, Palmer joined Chelsea, seeking regular first-team football. The move proved transformational for both player and club.

Breakthrough Season (2023-24)

Palmer's debut season at Chelsea was nothing short of spectacular. He became the first Chelsea player aged 21 or under to score 10 Premier League goals in a season, finishing with 22 goals and 11 assists across all competitions.

Record-Breaking Performances

• First hat-trick against Manchester United in a 4-3 victory
• Scored crucial penalty against former club Manchester City in 4-4 draw
• Became 13th player in Premier League history to achieve 20+ goals and 10+ assists in same season
• First player to win Goal of the Month award twice consecutively across two seasons

Contract Extension (2024)

Recognizing his exceptional talent, Chelsea secured Palmer's future with a nine-year contract extension, keeping him at Stamford Bridge until 2033.
